{% trans "Getting to Toronto" %}

{% blocktrans %} Toronto has 2 major airports. Pearson is the international airport and is the location for almost all the major domestic and international airlines. It's about a 35 minute drive from Pearson Airport to Chestnut Residence, but be aware that downtown Toronto traffic is busy all day and is particularly busy during peak rush hour. Allow at least an hour for travel time from the airport. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Porter Airlines has frequent, reasonably-priced flights for trips from several US cities to Toronto, including Boston, Burlington (VT), Chicago, Myrtle Beach, New York, and Washington. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} VIA is a good alternative to the airlines if you are coming from somewhere like Montreal or Ottawa. Advance pricing is often available at a discount. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} If you're coming from the US: {% endblocktrans %}
